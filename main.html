Cyber Security

Computer security, also known as cyber security or IT security, is the protection of computer systems from the theft or damage to their hardware, software or information, as well as from disruption or misdirection of the services they provide.

Hackers

A hacker is any skilled computer expert who uses his technical knowledge to gain unauthorized access to data.

Cyber Security-Best Practices

  1. Protect your personal information with strong passwords
  2. Use 2 Factor Authentication
  3. Use firewalls to ensure secure internet traffic
  4. Install antivirus on your computers and mobile devices
  5. Do not open mail attachments from unknown sources
